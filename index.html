Home Page Overview

Welcome

This site is a simple three page HTML5 website built for class. The goal is to practice structure, semantic elements, and linking between pages without worrying about design.

I’m building this like a mini professional homepage: who I am, what I’m about, and a few things I’m working on.

My main focus is learning by doing. If something breaks, I want to understand why it broke and how to fix it in a clean way instead of just guessing.

As the semester goes on, I’ll keep expanding the content so the pages feel more like a real portfolio rather than a template.
